Smartlink ELEC - TERMS OF USE

1.Introduction

Schneider Electric has developed Smartlink ELEC, a monitoring, alarming and control solution for electrical panel intended for professional who owns a business areas equipped with Schneider Electric's Smartlink ELEC system.

The Smartlink ELEC system consists of a set of equipments installed in the electrical cabinet of small tertiary buildings and sensors connected to Smartlink EL B product, as well as two Smartphone applications, Smartlink ELEC application for end-user, and the tool for the electrical cabinet installer.

The Smartlink ELEC application is available in Google Play or App Store and allows the occupants of a small tertiary to easily be aware of their shop or building energy consumption, switch the asset ON/OFF remotely or with pre-programmed schedule, as well as be alarmed of abnormal behaviors.
